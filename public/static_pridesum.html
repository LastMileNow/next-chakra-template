What can you do with us?

We help you create different kinds of Decentralized Autonomous Organizations (DAOs) depending on your goals.

  • PrideSeed

    Create new local advocacy groups with peer-to-peer advising and shared resources, passing on ownership from group to group around the world in a mentor-mentee relationship.

  • RainbowWave

    Fund LGBTQ+ political causes and have a say in how it's done, including specific causes to support, geographic region, and conditions of support.

  • Sanctuary

    Support and manage the operations of a social media platform for the LGBTQ+ community to talk openly and anonymously without fear of repercussions.

  • Pride Bot

    Finance the development of a Chatbot that can spread awareness of issues related to the stigmatization of LGBTQ+ topics and attempt to assist those in need of someone to talk to.

Features

DAOs can help your activism in several ways.

EQUITY

Create fair & sustainable organizations with DAOs.

Especially in the beginning of a social organization, issues of management and direction can plague its continued existence. However, the fundamental structure of a DAO remedies this through its intrinsically democratic nature, as well as specific resources and configurations provided by Pridesum.

  • Ownership is equal and customizable for advocacy applications.

  • Pridesum helps these DAOs develop, but only its members control it and can pass it on.

IMPACT

Spark a real impact in local communities.

Any organization needs know-how, motivation, and accessibility to be successful. Pridesum helps make that happen through its inherent incorporation of new technologies, including communications tools for conveying first-hand experience from more experienced activists and NFTs to provide recognition and documentation.

  • The virtual nature of DAOs can cross state and national lines.

  • Exclusive NFTs provide both record and incentive to make real change.
